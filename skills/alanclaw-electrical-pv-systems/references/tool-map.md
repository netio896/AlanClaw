# AlanClaw Electrical + PV Tool Map

## Electrical Design and Construction

Purpose:

- Organize low-voltage electrical design inputs.
- Prepare load schedule and panel coordination questions.
- Build preliminary BOQ checklists.
- Generate site inspection and construction sequence checklists.

Best for:

- Residential and small commercial electrical planning.
- Renovation electrical scope review.
- Panel and circuit planning discussions.
- Contractor/client coordination.

Recommended prompt:

```text
Use AlanClaw Electrical + PV Systems.

This is my electrical project input:
[paste load list, floor plan notes, panel details, or site observations]

Please output:
1. Scope summary
2. Missing design inputs
3. Preliminary electrical design checklist
4. BOQ pre-check table
5. Site inspection checklist
6. Client or electrician confirmation message

Mark all load, cable, breaker, grounding, safety, and price conclusions as "requires human confirmation".
```

## PV System Design and Construction

Purpose:

- Organize roof/site and PV target inputs.
- Prepare preliminary module, inverter, AC/DC route, and grid-connection checks.
- Build PV BOQ pre-checks and construction inspection steps.

Best for:

- Rooftop solar planning.
- Hybrid inverter or battery-ready planning.
- Site survey preparation.
- Utility/grid submission preparation.

Recommended prompt:

```text
Use AlanClaw Electrical + PV Systems.

This is my PV project input:
[paste roof area, site orientation, target kW, grid notes, photos summary, or equipment preference]

Please output:
1. PV scope summary
2. Missing site survey information
3. Preliminary PV design checklist
4. BOQ pre-check table
5. Construction and inspection sequence
6. Utility/client confirmation message

Mark all PV capacity, string sizing, inverter selection, cable, protection, generation, grid, price, and safety conclusions as "requires human confirmation".
```

## Combined Electrical + PV

Use this when PV ties into the building electrical system.

Focus areas:

- Existing incoming supply and panel capacity
- PV AC tie-in location
- Protection coordination
- Grounding/bonding and SPD
- Cable routes
- Utility meter and grid export rules
- Maintenance access and labeling

## Comparison

| Workflow | Input | Output | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Electrical | Floor plan, load list, panel info | Design checklist, BOQ pre-check, inspection checklist | Building electrical planning |
| PV | Roof/site notes, target kW, grid notes | PV checklist, BOQ pre-check, site survey questions | Solar planning |
| Combined | Electrical + PV inputs | Tie-in, protection, grounding, construction sequence | Grid-connected PV projects |
